About DashThing

DashThing is an open-source, customizable dashboard solution designed for both personal and team use. It lets you easily organize links, apps, and resources in one clean, dynamic interface.

DashThing can be configured via a simple config file, customize the layout, and create multiple pages to stay organized. DashThing offers a flexible, lightweight solution that adapts to your needs, making it ideal for both personal and team use.
